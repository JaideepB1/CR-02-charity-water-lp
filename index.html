Our Story

Charity: water partners with local leaders to build sustainable water projects rooted in transparency, community ownership, and long-term impact. We’re excited to continue empowering college students to help sustain these projects for years to come..

Real Impact: What do we do?

Charity: water empowers college students to help end the global water crisis by funding clean, life-changing water projects—with 100% of donations going directly to those in need. Together, we can turn compassion into action by creating a future where every community has access to safe water and the opportunity to thrive.
